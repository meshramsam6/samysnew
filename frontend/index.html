Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order on QuickBite Delivery?

To place an order, simply visit our website or open the app, browse through the list of available restaurants, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. You can choose your payment method and delivery address before confirming your order. It's that easy!

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the restaurant’s preparation time. On average, your order will arrive at the speed of light. You can track your order in real-time through our app or website to see the estimated arrival time.

Can I track my delivery in real-time?

Yes! You can track your delivery in real-time through the QuickBite Delivery app or website. You’ll be able to see the status of your order and know exactly when your food is on its way.

Do you offer contactless delivery?

Yes, we offer contactless delivery as an option. During checkout, you can select "Contactless Delivery" to ensure the driver leaves your order at the doorstep for a safe, hands-off experience.

Can I cancel or modify my order after it’s been placed?

Unfortunately, once your order is confirmed, it cannot be modified or canceled. However, you can contact our customer support team immediately, and we will try to assist you depending on the status of your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

e accept payments only via UPI (Unified Payments Interface). You can easily make your payment using any UPI-enabled app during the checkout process.
